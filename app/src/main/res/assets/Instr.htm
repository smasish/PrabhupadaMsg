Congratulations on purchasing the most convenient and mobile PMP® Course available today! BetterPM will help you get ready to take the challenging PMP® Exam.

If you have any questions at all, please use the contact button on the main course page,and a certified instuctor will get back to you within 24 hours.

Exam steps:

1. View the course videos. There are 11 in total, and each covers one or more chapters of the PMBOK® Guide

2. Take the 2 practice tests, Each one is 200 questions, and timed for 4 hours just like the real exam.You must get 70% of higher on the practice exams to pass.

3. Take the test for course completion. This is 50 questions. You must score 70% or higher in order to pass.

4. Once you pass the test for course completion, you will see an email dialogue box. Type in your name as you want to see it on the course completion certificate, and hit send. You will receive your course completion certificate within 2 business days.
